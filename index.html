The facts !

There is around 850 million users on Facebook

And 500 million on Twitter
only 140 million actives

People spend over 700 billion minutes per month on Facebook.
11 Twitter accounts created every second, that's 1 million accounts every day.
So what ?
  • 84% people "retweet" content from personal connection
  • 69% people start following from friends suggestions
  • Huge opportunities for social engineering
Socialbots
  • Post contents & send connections requests to real users
  • Influence a large and diverse population of web users
  • Interact with real users
Recognize bots (1/2)
  • Activity : Majority of fake profile didn't update their status.
  • Profile picture : Beautiful picture (mainly girls ...)
  • Pictures : Fake profile have 136 tags every 4 pictures (average) against only 1.
  • Information : gender, interest, education ...
Recognize bots (2/2)
  • Center of interest : Fake profiles don't have center of interests.
  • Number of friends : 726 for fake profile against 130 for real profile (Average)
  • Strange link in their posts : Advertisement, ...
  • Instant answer in message
Why attackers improve bots ?
  • Disign attractive messages / answer with link embedded.
  • People click on it because it's seems to be in their interest.
  • Install malware / divulge personal information.
Conclusion
  • Improve knowledge of people about the risk.
  • Improve detection of fake profiles in online social media website.
